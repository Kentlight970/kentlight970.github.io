This space covers the bottom of the screen. You arrange your video clips here in a left-to-right order. You can cut clips, remove parts you do not want, or change the duration of your images.

### The Effects Panel
Select effects from this menu. Click and drag an effect onto a clip in your timeline. The software will apply the change instantly.
